Cheesecake

Description:

Cheesecake is a dessert made with a soft fresh cheese (typically cottage cheese, cream cheese, quark or ricotta), eggs, and sugar. It may have a crust or base made from crushed cookies (or digestive biscuits), graham crackers, pastry, or sometimes sponge cake. Cheesecake may be baked or unbaked, and is usually refrigerated.

Required ingredients:

  • Cracker Crumbs
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cream Cheese
  • sour Cream
  • Vinilla
  • Eggs
  • Pie Filling

Cooking steps:

  1. Make the crust and press it into a springform pan.
  2. Make the filling, then pour it over the crust.
  3. Bake the cheesecake until the filling is almost set.
  4. Run a knife around the rim to loosen the sides and allow it to cool.
  5. Remove the cheesecake from the pan and chill before serving.
